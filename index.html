Ladder was inspired by midcentury menus, family diners, and art deco typography. I wanted each design choice to feel familiar but elevated—combining playful illustration, soft geometric forms, and a restrained palette that shifts with the time of day. The ladder anchors the brand to comfort food that carries you upward.

The visual identity of Ladder needed to match the restaurant’s comforting fare. My work included menu design, illustration, layout, and brand storytelling. Each menu—Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner, Drinks, and Dessert—features a custom graphic treatment and color palette, all tied together by a recurring ladder motif that reinforces the brand’s name.
